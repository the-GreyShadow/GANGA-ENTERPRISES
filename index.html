About Us

Our Story

Mrs. Punam Tiwary & Mr. Manoj Kumar Tiwary jointly evolved with an idea of generating a support system to organizations struggling for manpower and people looking for employment. This vision birthed Ganga Enterprise, which has grown from a local presence to serving 20+ Organizations Pan India.

Mission

To be our customer's trusted partner, delivering total business support solutions that enable our customers to focus on their core business while we handle their manpower needs with excellence.

Vision

To prosper as an entity and be a partner in helping India emerge as an enriching economical power through strategic manpower solutions and employment generation.
